To individual entrepreneurs and organizations Cucak.am offers free space and an address, which can serve as a personal site (e.g. laptopshop.cucak.am). This will save time, nerves, and the extra money that would be spent on development of the private Web site and maintenance.

ATTENTION: The following offers are only for registered users.

Collaboration

Starting from 10 announcements the link to your page can be shown in Partners section of the Main page and in Partners' pyramid for free. The frequency in which it is displayed and how high it is in Partners' pyramid is directly proportional to your index. (If you wish your page to be displayed in this manner, please let us know in written form.)

Announcements added on your page will also appear on corresponding section of Cucak.am. So your visitors will find you through your page and as well as by browsing announcements on Cucak.am.

Provision of sub-addresses

Starting from 20 announcements you are freely given the ability to get a sub-address: led.cucak.am, which will be connected to the your page: cucak.am/263.

(We also freely provide the connection of your preordered domain to your site, e.g. notebookcentre.am).

The address must consist of your name or the name of your organization and should not include a known brand name if you don't have permission for it.

Requests for private addresses can be sent to the following email address support@cucak.am
